Sundance Film Festival, January 2011


As part of the Sundance Festival, Lynn's groundbreaking new media installation RAW/WAR debuted at New Frontier, which is an "experiment in festival presentation" social and creative space that showcases media installations, multimedia performances, transmedia experiences, panel discussions, and more." Created in collaboration with !W.A.R. producer Alexandra Chowaniec, filmmaker and technologist Brian Chirls, Paradiso Projects and consultant Gian Pablo Villami, RAW/WAR reconceives history as an ongoing project of collaborative authorship where participants enter an interactive environment to upload new material and use Wii powered virtual flashlights to illuminate invisible histories.
